

# E-commerce Sales Analysis Report

## 1. Introduction

This project focuses on analyzing e-commerce sales data using Python. The objective is to understand sales performance, identify top-selling products, and analyze revenue distribution across different categories. The project follows a complete data analysis pipeline including data loading, cleaning, analysis, and visualization.

---

## 2. Objectives

* To calculate total revenue generated from sales
* To identify the best-selling product based on quantity
* To analyze sales distribution across categories
* To visualize data using charts for better understanding
* To generate meaningful insights for decision-making

---

## 3. Dataset Description

The dataset contains sales transaction records with the following columns:

* Date: The date of sale
* Product: Name of the product
* Category: Product category (Electronics, Fashion, Accessories)
* Quantity: Number of units sold
* Price: Price per unit

---

## 4. Tools and Technologies Used

* Python (Programming Language)
* Pandas (Data analysis and manipulation)
* Matplotlib (Data visualization)

---

## 5. Data Loading and Cleaning

The dataset was loaded using pandas. Several data cleaning steps were performed:

* Removed duplicate rows to avoid incorrect analysis
* Handled missing values by replacing them with 0
* Cleaned column names by removing extra spaces
* Verified data types for accuracy

These steps ensured that the dataset was consistent and ready for analysis.

---

## 6. Data Analysis

A new column called Total_Sales was created using the formula:

Total_Sales = Quantity × Price

The following key metrics were calculated:

* Total Revenue: Sum of all sales values
* Best-Selling Product: Product with highest quantity sold
* Sales by Category: Total revenue generated by each category

---

## 7. Data Visualization

Two types of charts were created to better understand the data:

### Bar Chart

The bar chart shows revenue generated by each category. It helps compare performance across different categories such as Electronics, Fashion, and Accessories.

### Pie Chart

The pie chart shows the percentage contribution of each category to total sales. It provides a clear view of which category dominates overall sales.

---

## 8. Results

* Total Revenue: ₹1,78,000
* Best-Selling Product: T-Shirt
* Highest Revenue Category: Electronics

---

## 9. Insights

* Electronics products generate the highest revenue due to higher prices
* Fashion products sell in higher quantities but contribute less revenue
* Accessories have the lowest contribution to total sales
* High-priced products significantly impact total revenue

---

## 10. Conclusion

The analysis provides a clear understanding of sales performance across different categories. While some products sell more frequently, others generate higher revenue. This information can help businesses focus on high-performing categories and improve overall sales strategies.
